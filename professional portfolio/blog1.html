Today AI on the peak

  • shahroz
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • 14

The term Artificial Intelligence was first coined in 1956 by computer scientist John McCarthy. AI is defined as "the science and engineering of making intelligent machines, especially intelligent computer programs." Over the past few decades, there has been significant progress in the field of AI, and it is now being used in a variety of industries, including healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and logistics.

AI can be divided into three different categories: narrow AI, general AI, and super AI.

Narrow AI

This type of AI is also known as weak AI or applied AI. It is designed to perform a specific task, such as facial recognition or voice recognition

General AI

This type of AI is also known as strong AI or artificial general intelligence. It has the ability to learn and work on any task that a human can do.

Super AI

This is the highest level of AI, where the machine has the ability to surpass human intelligence.
At present, most businesses are using narrow AI to automate tasks and improve efficiency. However, with the rapid advancement of technology, it is expected that businesses will soon start using general AI and super AI to transform their operations.
